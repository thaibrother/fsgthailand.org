6 work groups serving vulnerable communities and promoting justice and peace

Overview

The Commission on Activities & JPIC (Justice, Peace, Integrity of Creation) coordinates the Foundation's social mission, pastoral presence, and commitment to human dignity and environmental stewardship. Grounded in Catholic social teaching and the Montfort charism.

Social Welfare

งานสังคมสงเคราะห์ — Direct services to vulnerable populations, emergency assistance, shelter, and livelihood support programs.

Justice, Peace & Human Rights

งานเสริมสร้างความยุติธรรม สันติ และส่งเสริมสิทธิมนุษยชน — Advocacy for justice, conflict resolution, and promotion of human dignity.

Integrity of Creation

งานบูรณภาพของสิ่งสร้าง — Environmental stewardship, sustainability initiatives, and ecological consciousness-raising.

Pastoral Care

งานอภิบาล — Spiritual accompaniment, counseling, religious education, and faith community building.

Activities & Recreation

งานกิจกรรมและนันทนาการ — Student activities, cultural programs, athletics, and youth engagement initiatives.

Legal Matters

งานด้านกฎหมาย — Legal counsel, contracts, compliance, and protection of institutional rights.
