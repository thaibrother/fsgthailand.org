About the Foundation

Who We Are

A Catholic educational foundation operating under Thai law, rooted in 120 years of Montfortian tradition

The Foundation

The Foundation of the Brothers of Saint Gabriel in Thailand is a legally registered educational foundation under Thai law. It oversees 14 schools across the nation, serving tens of thousands of students from kindergarten through secondary and technical education. The Foundation operates under the spiritual and educational guidance of the Congregation of the Brothers of Saint Gabriel, headquartered in Rome.

Our Heritage

The Congregation was founded in 1711 by Saint Louis-Marie Grignion de Montfort in France. His vision of holistic education — forming the whole person in mind, body, and spirit — has been carried by the Brothers of Saint Gabriel across more than 40 countries. In 1901, the first Brothers arrived in Siam and established what would become Assumption College, the oldest school in our network.

Governance

The Foundation Board of Directors is chaired by the Provincial Superior of the Thai Province, with Provincial Councilors serving as ex-officio board members. The Provincial Council, based at Soi Thonglor in Bangkok, provides strategic direction for all 14 schools, ensuring alignment with Montfortian values, Thai educational standards, and international quality frameworks.

Core Values

Truth (Veritas) — A relentless pursuit of integrity and knowledge.
Service (Servitium) — The selfless dedication to the common good, inspired by the spirit of Saint Louis-Marie de Montfort.
Excellence (Prestantia) — Upholding the standard of “Labor Omnia Vincit” in every dimension of life.

SWIS — The Digital Pulse of Montfortian Education

School Web-based Information System — our cloud-native ecosystem for data-driven education management

The Foundation’s Central Intelligence

SWIS is our proprietary, cloud-native ecosystem developed by SWIS Plus Co. It serves as the foundation’s central intelligence, seamlessly integrating school management, academic tracking, and administrative workflows into a unified, web-based platform. Designed for the 21st-century educator, it ensures that data-driven decision-making is accessible anytime, anywhere.

With over 220 functional modules serving all 14 schools, SWIS connects job specifications to real performance, tracks staff competencies and workload, and powers everything from quality assurance (PDCA) to executive dashboards — aligned with Pillar 4: Digital Empowerment & Infrastructure.
